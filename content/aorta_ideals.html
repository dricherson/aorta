Ideals
Consent, Communication, and Contribution
Everyone twenty-one years of age and older are encouraged to participate in the community. Fostering growth in each other through acceptance in a consent based culture where all may feel safe to explore themselves and the beauty around them with no fear of violence or sexual assault. A.O.R.T.A., LLC. welcomes all participants over the age of twenty-one and does not discriminate on the basis of gender, sexual orientation, disability, religious affiliation, race or socioeconomic status.
 
Generosity, Respect, and Individuality
Our event strives to promote and support personal growth by honoring the individual diversity within each of us and celebrating those differences. We give freely of ourselves without expectation in order to embrace the here and now. We honor ourselves and others by donating our time, our art, and our support to each other.
 
Market Independence, Self Sufficiency, and Leave No Trace
In the spirit of self sufficiency our event shys away from market independence and corporate sponsorships. Asking instead that members bring all that they need to sustain themselves, and that we leave the land better than it was found through strict leave no trace policies.
Don't Be A Douche
Don't let your self expression negatively impact others.

Mission Statement
To promote and foster the finite, static, and industrial arts by creating an environment for the creation of art. To build a community that values diversity and engages in the creative design process, to provide the opportunity by which people can explore, learn and exchange the industrial artisanal skills in an adults only environment.
 
To develop, educate and support a community of servant leaders in an adults only environment through interpersonal cooperation, providing volunteer leadership training, and encouraging conflict resolution.
 
To establish an adults only, participant-based community that promotes social and civic responsibility within the participant- created community and society at large, by developing sustainable processes that empower others to spread these Ideals both within the community based event as well as our local communities outside the event.

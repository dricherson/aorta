Adults Only Recreational Thermodynamic Adventure (A.O.R.T.A.) is Wichita's newest, and only, ADULTS ONLY camping event. It is our goal to create an open and accepting community of people from every walk of life, who strive to build each other up and support one another through ACCEPTANCE, ART in all its forms and MUSIC. Our community is centered on 10 basic IDEALS that are meant to help us grow as individuals and as a community both inside the event as well as in our daily lives.

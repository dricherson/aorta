Volunteer Positions
Band-aid Brigade
Provide basic first aid. Band aids and boo boos.
 
Education
Welcome new attendees and provide information on the rules and ideals of the event.
 
Effigy
Folks that help design and assemble the effigy and temple (if there is one).
 
F.A.T.S.
Our workhorses basically. They help build and tear down infrastructure, help large camps gather firewood for infrastructure. Lots of sweat and heavy lifting.
 
FAST
Our fire safety team.
 
Gate and Parking
Registration. Verifies folks and signs them in. Keeps parking organized and helping people take stuff to their camps.
 
LNT
Leave No Trace - volunteers pick up trash, clean bathrooms, changes out tp.
 
Procession
Helps with scheduling and coordinating the pre burn performances.
 
Rangers
Sort of like security but more low key...they keep an eye on things and provide help when and where needed. They help de-escalate negative situations and are there to help those who need it.
 
Sanctuary
Sanctuary are part of the medics, but they are medics for the mind. Sometimes things get overwhelming and they are calm safe space.

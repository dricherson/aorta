A.O.R.T.A. (Adults Only Recreational Thermodynamic Adventure) is and adults only, art, music and self expression event near the Wichita area.
Hot food and cold drinks will be not available for sale.
2018 A.O.R.T.A.
Adults Only Recreational Thermodynamic AdventureEvent
Date: August 24th, 25th and 26th, 2018
Location: Whispering Winds Camp, Valley Center, Kansas
Participants: Adults over the age of 21.
Tickets: $40 for the first 120 tickets (founders) and $50 for the remaining 380 tickets.
